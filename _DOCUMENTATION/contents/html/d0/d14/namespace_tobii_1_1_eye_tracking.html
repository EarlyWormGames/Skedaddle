◆ GazeTrackingStatus

enum Tobii.EyeTracking.GazeTrackingStatus
strong

Represents different gaze tracking states.

Enumerator
Unknown 

Gaze tracking is unknown. This might be due to an error.

GazeTracked 

The user's eye-gaze is tracked. This means that gaze point data is delivered from the eye tracker.

GazeNotTracked 

The user's eye-gaze is not tracked. This means that the eye tracker does not deliver gaze point data.

NotSupported 

Gaze tracking state is not supported in the version of Tobii Engine that the user has.
